Clue 3: The Fibonacci sequence leads to a mathematical ratio used by Da Vinci.

Using the Fibonacci sequence, take the ratio of consecutive numbers and approximate the famous mathematical ratio Da Vinci used in his art. What is this ratio?

Here are the first 6 numbers in the Fibonacci sequence:

1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8

Now, divide each consecutive pair of numbers and observe the result. What famous mathematical ratio does this resemble?

Hint: This ratio is known for its connection to aesthetics and nature, often called the "divine proportion".

Correct! The ratio is the Golden Ratio (1.618), also called the Divine Proportion, used by Da Vinci in his works. Let's uncover the final treasure!
